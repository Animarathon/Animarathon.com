What is Animarathon?

Animarathon is a family friendly convention that celebrates Anime, Japanese Media and Nerd Culture at Bowling Green State University in the Bowen Thompson Student Union.

Events at Animarathon include panels, cosplay contests, an artist's alley, anime screenings, special guests, tabletop and video games, a manga library, a rave, and much more.

Animarathon XV will be held on March 25th 2017 from 9:00AM to Midnight.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Prices

  • $7 for BGSU students
  • $10 for general admission at the door
  • $15 for pre registration which guarantees your badge, and carries other perks.

The price for Animarathon has raised in 2017 as preparations are underway to make Animarathon a two day event in 2018, and funding is needed to make it a reality.

The Pre-Registration price will rise to $20 in 2018. Otherwise the price of Animarathon will not rise more then this for the foreseeable future.
