About Animarathon!

Animarathon is a family friendly celebration of Anime, Japanese Media and Nerd Culture at Bowling Green State University in the Bowen Thompson Student Union. Animarathon XV will be held on March 25th 2017 from 9:00AM to Midnight.

We look forward to seeing you there!
