What is Animapathon?

Animapathon was a free educational mapathon held at Bowling Green State University in Room 208 of the Bowen Thompson Student Union on November 15th 2016 from 5:00PM to 7:30PM.

Animapathon was a charitable sister event of the Animarathon anime convention and part of the OSM Geography Awareness Week.

Animapathon covered a non-technical introduction to OpenStreetMap and related communities.

Animapathon Results

Animapathon I focused on Toledo Ohio and Dinsoor Somolia. Participents learned about OpenStreetMap, and additionally a major Toledo park and several features elsewhere were mapped.
