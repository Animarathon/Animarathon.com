About Animapathon!

Animapathon is a free educational mapathon at Bowling Green State University in the Bowen Thompson Student Union Room 208 on November 15th 2016 from 5:00PM to 7:30PM.

Animapathon is a charitable sister event of the Animarathon anime convention and part of the OSM Geography Awareness Week.

Animapathon covers a non-technical introduction to OpenStreetMap, how to make your first contribution, aiding disaster prone areas with mapping and much more.

We look forward to seeing you there!

Recommended Materials

We encourage you to bring a laptop and a mouse or smartphone, but if you don't have a portable computer you are still encouraged to attend!
