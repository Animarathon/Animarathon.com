What is Animapathon?

Animapathon was a free educational mapathon held at Bowling Green State University in the Bowen Thompson Student Union Room 208 on November 15th 2016 from 5:00PM to 7:30PM.

Animapathon was a charitable sister event of the Animarathon anime convention and part of the OSM Geography Awareness Week.

Animapathon covered a non-technical introduction to OpenStreetMap.

Animapathon Results

Animapathon I focused on Toledo Ohio and Dinsoor Somolia mapping a major toledo park and several features elsewhere.
