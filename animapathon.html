Animapathon: You can be a hero!

Animapathon II is a free educational mapathon held at Bowling Green State University in Room 207 of the Bowen Thompson Student Union on April 13th 2017 from 5:00PM to 7:00PM.

Animapathon II will a non-technical introduction to OpenStreetMap and related communities.

Animapathon Goals

Animapathon II will focus on Perrysburg Ohio. Participents will learn about OpenStreetMap.
