What Is ANO?

ANO, short for "Anime In Northwest Ohio" is the offical Anime student organization of Bowling Green State University in Bowluing Green Ohio.

ANO membership is free and open to anyone. and a great way to unwind after a long week of classes.

ANO membership is also one of the ultimate ways to learn about and get involved in planning our anual anime convention, Animarathon.

Meetings

Regular meetings are held every Friday from 7:00PM to 11:00PM in the Bowen Thompson Student Union located on the main BGSU campus. Each meeting is packed with a screening of five to six anime episodes, and various activities afterwords ranging from fanfiction contests to playing obscure video games.

At the begining of each academic semester, a special meeting is held where club members vote on the anime that we will watch for the rest of the semester.
