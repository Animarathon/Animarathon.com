Animarathon Panels

Animarathon panelists cover a wide range of subjects from Anime to Zombies. Come back later for Animarathon XVI Panel Submissions!

What is a panel?

A panel is a type of event that is meant to be informative on a certain topic. Unlike lectures, Panels are often interactive, and can cover a wide variety of topics.

Past Animarathon panels have covered a multitude of topics, including introductions to specific series, informational sessions on Japanese Culture, Q&A with celebrity guests, introduction to game design, discussion of Internet culture, and more!

Ratings Guide

  • Everyone

    • Suitable for all ages. This panel contains no swearing and no adult content.

  • Teen

    • Suitable for ages 13 and up. This panel contains swearing or alludes to adult content.

  • Mature

    • Suitable for ages 18 and up. This panel contains swearing or uncensored adult content.

    We require a Photo ID that shows your date of birth to enter Mature panels.
