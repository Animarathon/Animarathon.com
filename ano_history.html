History of ANO

Although ANO documents reference dates as old as 1996, Anime In Northwest Ohio became a official Bowling Green State University student organization in Febuary of 2002. Meeting in the then brand new Bowen Thompson Student Union Theater, ANO members would watch their favorite animes. Satisfied with what they had created, they celebrated just over one year later, holding the first Animarathon. Since then ANO has continued to watch Anime and host Animarathon.
