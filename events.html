Animarathon Events

The full Animarathon XVI event listing has not yet been published. We hope our basic listing can tide you over!

Raffle

The Animarathon Raffle provides a variety of themed prizes. Past Animarathons have provided a variety of prizes including Animes, Mangas, Games, Game consoles, and more!

Raffle Ticket Sales will be open until 8:30PM on Saturday

The raffle drawing will be from 9:00PM to 10:00PM Saturday in the Falcons Nest on the First floor of the Union.

Dealers Room & Artists Alley

Have a Ball in our Ballroom of Artists and Merchants, selling awesome and hard to get Anime merchandise and custom artwork!

General Access: 11:00AM-6:00AM

Pre-Registration Exclusive Access: 10:00AM-11:00AM on Saturday

Manga Library

Never had an opportunity to enjoy manga before? Are you a manga aficionado looking for a rare manga volume? The Manga Library has what you're looking for! Take a break by reading some manga in our well stocked library!

The Manga Library will be in Room 318 from 9:00AM to 10:00PM on Saturday and 9:00AM-5:30PM Sunday.

Animarathon Rave

Dance the night away as the night draws to a close!

The rave will be from 9:00PM to Midnight on Saturday.
