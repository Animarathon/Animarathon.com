Animarathon Events

The full Animarathon XV event listing has not yet been published. We hope our basic listing can tide you over!

Kawaii Kafé

Kawaii Kafé provides a relaxed and themed atmosphere to kick back and take a break from the con. Enjoy cakes, sandwiches, and more from our lovely servers!

The Kawaii Kafé Menu

  • Chibi Veggie Wraps*^
  • Moe Meat Sandwitches* (Ham and Turkey)
  • Strawberry Fudge Cake
  • Raspberry Fudge Cake
  • Inari
  • California Roll
  • Vegetable Roll
  • Vegetable Combo Roll
  • Ice Cold Lemonaide
  • Hot Herbed Tea
  • Ice Cold Water

* Indicates a vegetarian dish.

^ Indicates a gluten free option on request.

Raffle

The Animarathon Raffle provides a variety of themed prizes. Past Animarathons have provided a variety of prizes including Animes, Mangas, Games, Game consoles, and more!

Raffle Ticket Sales will be open until 8:30

The raffle drawing will be from 9:00PM to 10:00PM in the multipurpose room.

Erie Retrocade

Yern for the old days of retro gaming? Erie Retrocade from Erie Pensylvania is an interactive gaming museum, and will be visiting Animarathon XV! Experience a multitude of classic games and systems!

Retrocade is in room 308 and is open from 9AM to Midnight.

Video Game Room

BGSU's very own Gaming Society will be helping provide a game room! Enjoy the best of cutting edge modern gaming!

The Gaming Society rooms will be on the third floor, and open from 9AM to Midnight.

Tabletop Gaming Room

BGSU's very own Gaming Society will be helping provide a tabletop gaming room! Enjoy the best of tabletop gaming and roll some nat 20's!

The Gaming Society rooms will be on the third floor, and open from 9AM to Midnight.

Dealers Room

Have a Ball in our Ballroom of Artists and Merchants, selling awesome and hard to get Anime merchandise and custom artwork!

General Access: 11:00AM-6:00AM

Pre-Registration Exclusive Access: 10:00AM-11:00AM

Manga Library

Never had an opportunity to enjoy manga before? Are you a manga aficionado looking for a rare manga volume? The Manga Library has what you're looking for! Take a break by reading some manga in our well stocked library!

The Manga Library will be in Room 318 from 9:00AM to 10:00PM.

Game and Anime Music Ensemble

BGSU's all-star music ensemble G.A.M.E. makes a comeback to deliver only the highest quality video game and anime music covers!

GAME will be preforming in the Multipurpose Room from 1:00PM to 4:00PM.

Ghost In Blue

Listen to some great J-Rock and anime music preformed by Ghost In Blue!

Ghost In Blue will be preforming in the Multipurpose Room from 7:00PM to 8:30PM.

Animarathon Rave

Dance the night away as the convention draws to a close! Featuring DJ Marson, Adinga, and Haji.

The rave will be in the Ballroom from 9:00PM to Midnight.
